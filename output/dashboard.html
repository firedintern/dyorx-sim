Avg Payout Received ($)

What this shows The average amount the payout recipient received each round (full pool balance at time of payout). In a ROSCA, if members drop out or skip, the pot shrinks — so payout quality is a direct function of circle health. DYORX payouts are slightly higher overall because more members contributed more consistently. The Tough Circle shows the clearest damage: $1,474 avg payout vs $1,978 in the Best Case.

Dropout Timing (avg round)

What this shows The average round number at which dropouts occurred. Later is better — it means the circle stayed intact longer before anyone quit. DYORX delayed attrition by 1.5 rounds on average (round 7.6 vs 6.1). This matters because early dropouts cascade: one person quitting in round 3 shrinks the pot for everyone and can trigger panic in social followers. DYORX's yield signal keeps members engaged through the critical early rounds.
